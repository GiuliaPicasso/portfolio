Life of koi




A brief pop-up book that offers an easy way to learn about the world of one of Japan’s most representative animals: “koi” (鯉) carp. Through colorful illustrations, koi carps, are first introduced, then moving on to a rapid exploration of the places where they grow, their appearance and internal structure, their way of socializing and what they eat. After a focused research and visit to Niigata Prefecture, I first drew sketches of koi using pencils and watercolours. The final illustrations were then made using Procreate on iPad.
