RePlastic House




Designed to meet the needs of a young and environmentally conscious target audience, the residence consists of three micro rooms with maximum functionality, a multi-zone independent bathroom, and a dynamic and reconfigurable living area and kitchen. Outside, there is a space for plastic collection and beehives. The structure allows light and greenery to permeate, which is technologically reproduced inside through hydroponic cultivation.

The model has been designed to be disassembled into its components, in order to enhance the functional modules it is composed of. Its aesthetics reflect the essence of the concept, simplicity and sustainability, through the choice of neutral colors for the structure that in turn enhance the unique points of color provided by some designed furnishings.
