The Secret Twines

POSTER FOR INTERNO VERDE'S 2022 FESTIVAL IN FERRARA

Presented as a small theater full of unexpected events, The Secret Twines offers an unprecedented point of view on the Hirsch House, focusing on the importance of the stories that have lived through it. The villa and its garden were the setting for a multiple intertwining of lives, which can still be told: stories of stays and escapes, defeats and victories, mutual help and great hopes. Everything is kept hidden in the “false well”, a silent beating heart in the center of the garden and a key element of the visual story.
Born as a secret passage to reach the basement of the villa, a place where many Jews took refuge during racial persecutions, today it is the spokesperson for those stories. In the darkness of the night and framed by the architecture, the vivid past of the place comes to life from the well: an intertwining of golden flowers that bloom, illuminating the garden with a new and magical atmosphere.
