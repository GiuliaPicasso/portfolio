Typeface Welding Manual




A manual that promises anyone the ability to build, or rather weld (Yōsetsu, よせつ), their own original typographic character. In the first pages there is a study of the characters depicted on Italian shop signs. Subsequently, taking my own minimal knowledge of katakana as a prerequisite, the project changes direction towards the signs of Japanese shops to identify similarities and differences. The aim of “welding” the two identified styles together to create a third, eccentric final style.
